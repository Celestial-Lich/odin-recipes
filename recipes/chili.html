Delicious Chili

This chili is quick and very easy to make, also very inexpensive, makes enough to feed 3 people over the course of 3 days.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cans chili beans
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can tomatoes green chilis
  • 2 cans beef broth
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • salt/pepper
  • half a cup of sugar
  • rosemary
  • cayenne
  1. In a large pot, brown the ground beef and mix in onion add spices.
  2. Once meat is browned, add in the rest of ingredients, bring to a boil and add spices again.
  3. Turn heat to low and simmer for at least 15 mins.
  4. Serve with crackers or cornbread.
